Frequently Asked Questions

What is Linea pricing based on?

Our pricing reflects the action we help brands take through measurement. The value of our service is realised through increases in new customers, sales, and profit. When working with partners, we typically expect to be able to drive up to a 25 percent uplift in CPA or ROI.

What factors influence Linea pricing?

Prices shown on this page are indicative. Final pricing depends on your specific requirements. This includes the number of models, KPIs, and markets being measured. Additional analysis, such as brand awareness, consideration, or the causal impact of Paid Search, can also influence the final scope and price.

How is Linea MMM so much faster than traditional methods?

We are built for the speed of modern marketing. We achieve this through faster data access via APIs and a transparent, platform-led delivery. This makes MMM easier to run, more efficient, and enables you to make decisions sooner.

Why do Linea include factors such as price, seasonality and market in your models?

We control for all relevant factors before isolating the incremental impact of marketing. Without this, many measurement approaches overestimate marketing impact when performance may have been driven by seasonality, price changes, or shifts in the competitive landscape.

What media channels do Linea measure?

We measure all marketing channels across the full mix.

  • Brand and performance media.
  • Digital and offline channels, including TV and outdoor.
  • Paid and owned media.

Where relevant, we deep dive into channel tactics such as creative and placement. This allows us to provide an unbiased and comprehensive view of marketing impact.
