Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need for this to work?

The Linea Scenario Tool uses your existing media response curves from your in house or agency MMM. We would need the adstock, diminishing returns, last 12 months spend (by week) and ROIs.

How do I feed in data from Robyn & Meridian?

The Linea team support you on onboarding. We supply a standardised template that takes ~5 mins to complete. This feeds the tool. To ensure accuracy the Linea team review this to allow you to run scenarios with confidence.

We are currently building API connections to allow automated updates.

How do I ensure accuracy of the Linea Scenario Tool?

The tool is using data built from your in house or agency MMM. Where this is a concern for teams we recommend the “Linea Scenario Tool & Model Review”.

This ensures experienced modellers provide input focused on how to improve, build confidence and take action from your MMM model.

How do we overlay market or economy factors?

This interconnectivity needs to be measured in your in house or agency MMM. If it has been measured then it will be updated via the template data upload.
